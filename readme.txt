Here you can find the exact meaning of each column and a link to the source. 

1.MOEXRUB - MOEX stock price in RUB(https://www.moex.com/en/index/IMOEX/archive/#/from=1997-09-01&till=2019-01-01&sort=TRADEDATE&order=desc (monthly))
2.RUSCPI - Russia CPI 2015=100 (https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/RUSCPIALLMINMEI (monthly))
3.USCPI - U.S. CPI 2015=100 (https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/CPALTT01USM661S (monthly))
4.USDRUB - USD/RUB historical (https://uk.investing.com/currencies/usd-rub-historical-data (monthly))
5.WTIUSD - West Texas Intermediate (WTI) crude oil price in dollars (https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/MCOILWTICO (monthly))
6.CRUDEPROD - Monthly global oil production data in thousand barrels per day (https://www.eia.gov/totalenergy/data/browser/?tbl=T11.01B#/?f=M (monthly))
7.ACTIVITY - Global index of dry cargo single voyage freight rates (https://sites.google.com/site/lkilian2019/research/data-sets (monthly))
